  • Some stuff I've done

    Swift Innovators' Summit Alumni

    I made, developed and presented Fractal at the inaugral Swift Innovators' Summit in 2018. Fractal was later published on the App Store.

    I also got the opportunity to speak and present to Tim Cook - CEO of Apple Inc. (I'm now one of the few people in the world to have beaten him at whack-a-mole, and maybe the only one to have beaten him in VR whack-a-mole.)

    Getting to see new batches of earnest students harness the power of app development always warms my heart as an alumni of the Swift Accelerator Programme.

    Keyboard click-clack

    I've been invested in the keyboard hobby since 2020 (when the COVID-19 pandemic hit), and every step on this journey to mastery of this hobby has been fun and fulfilling.

    Helping others with accurate information and an open mind has always been one of my focuses - both in the hobby and in real life.

    I also specialise in assembling luxury keyboard kits for customers, tuning and building them to as close as perfect can get.
